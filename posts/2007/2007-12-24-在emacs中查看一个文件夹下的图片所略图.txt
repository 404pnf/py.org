在emacs中查看一个文件夹下的图片所略图

>q: I have a bunch of images in a dir. Is there a way to get a overview? > In emacs version 22, use Alt+x tumme. (“tumme” means thumb in Swedish. You need ImageMagick installed for tumme to work.). Emacs will create thumbnails on the fly. Here's a screenshot: > http://xahlee.org/emacs/emacs\_html.html
