## William Z. Shetter's writing on language

>Mini-essays about human language in its endless kaleidoscope of aspects, such as
the social, the mental, the historical, the structural

>A new essay will appear on this page around the first of every month.

>These Miniatures are all written and illustrated especially for presentation on this website.
