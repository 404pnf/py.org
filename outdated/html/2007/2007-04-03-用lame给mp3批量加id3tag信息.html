ID3 comments switches parameters –tt “title” title of song –ta “artist” artist who did the song –tl “album” album where it came from –ty “year” year in which the song/album was made –tc “comment” additional info –tn “track” track number of the song on the CD (1 to 255, creates an ID3 v 1.1 tag) –tg “genre” genre of song (name or number)
