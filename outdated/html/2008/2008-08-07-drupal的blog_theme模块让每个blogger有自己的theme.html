drupal的blog theme模块让每个blogger有自己的theme

以前用drupal做社区，特别是提供每个用户自己blog的类似社区时，用户最大的要求就是想要自己的theme。就是自己的blog有自己特定的模板。现在ddrupal这个模块提供了这个功能。

Blog Theme

Community · Content display · Theme related · Modules beginner - March 22, 2005 - 10:29

Blog theme allows users to have persistent themes for their blogs based on the theme they choose for their account. When others view thier main blog page, or any node (of specific admin-defined types) created by them, the reader will see the authors theme instead of their own.

The module maintainer is happy to sponsor the following non-profit charitable web site: Activist Solutions, Reuniting, Overshoot TV, minguo.info, We Change .org. http://drupal.org/project/blogtheme
