don有穿戴（wear）的意思

wear: put clothing on one’s body; “What should I wear today?”; “He put on his best suit for the wedding”; “The princess donned a long blue dress”; “The queen assumed the stately robes”; “He got into his jeans”

这个解释排在最后看来不是很常用。但我今天还是看见了。所以记录一下。一般出现在上下文中，做穿戴讲是肯定能猜到的。比如我今天看到的是： “I would have donned my best tie to see you.”

  • a Spanish gentleman or nobleman
  • preceptor: teacher at a university or college (especially at Cambridge or Oxford)
  • the head of an organized crime family
  • Celtic goddess; mother of Gwydion and Arianrhod; corresponds to Irish Danu
  • a European river in southwestern Russia; flows into the Sea of Azov
  • a Spanish courtesy title or form of address for men that is prefixed to the forename; “Don Roberto”
