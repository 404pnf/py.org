drupal中方便每个用户查看自己文章留言的模块

给每个用户提供一个页面，上面只显示他写的文章的新留言，而不是全站所有的新留言。 这个模块还没有port到6.0

http://drupal.org/project/comments_page

Comments Page

Content display · Modules mjmalloy - October 28, 2007 - 20:55

The Comments Page module will display all comments, or a certain user’s comments, on a page similar to how nodes are listed; that is, comments will be listed like nodes – with a title, date, user, and comment body – and then they are paginated.

Access all comments by visiting http://example.com/comments Access a user’s comments by visiting http://example.com/comments/xxx … Where example.com is your website and xxx is the user ID.
