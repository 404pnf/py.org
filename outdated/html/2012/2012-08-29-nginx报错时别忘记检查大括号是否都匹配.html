nginx报错时别忘记检查大括号是否都匹配

今天为了一个小问题浪费了20分钟

sudo /usr/local/nginx/sbin/nginx -t nginx: [emerg] “server” directive is not allowed here in /usr/local/nginx/conf/sites-list.conf:10

怎么看都没有错误。最后发现是大括号没匹配。

第二个错误 sudo /usr/local/nginx/sbin/nginx -t nginx: [emerg] the INET6 sockets are not supported on this platform in “[::]:80” of the “listen” directive in /usr/local/nginx/conf/sites-list.conf:12

这个简单。该机器不支持ipv6地址。改 [::]:80 为 80 就好了。
