21600首进入共有领域的英文歌谣： Roud Folk Song Index

The Roud Folk Song Index is a database of 300,000 references to over 21,600 songs that have been collected from oral tradition in the English language from all over the world. It is compiled by Steve Roud, a former librarian in the London Borough of Croydon http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roud_Folk_Song_Index
